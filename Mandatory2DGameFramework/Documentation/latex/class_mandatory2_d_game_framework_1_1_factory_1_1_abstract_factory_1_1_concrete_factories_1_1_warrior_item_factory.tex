\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{namespace_mandatory2_d_game_framework_1_1_factory}{Factory} for creating Warrior-\/themed equipment. Creates heavy Plate armor and melee Sword weapons. 

This factory produces a family of items suitable for Warrior class characters\+: 
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\bfseries{Weapon\+:}} Sword (high damage, melee range) 
\item {\bfseries{Armor\+:}} Plate material (highest defense values) 
\end{DoxyItemize}

Default values\+: Weapon damage 90, armor defense ranges from 6-\/20 depending on slot.
